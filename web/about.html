About

Campfyre is an online message board that allows anyone to post without having to worry about accounts or passwords. Campfyre also limits posts and comments to 256 characters to ensure a quick discussion platform.

Campfyre uses 'robofaces' to make following posts easier. These faces are generated based on a hash of your IP address. Every IP address has a roboface so if someone posts/comments from the same location more than once they will (usually) have the same roboface.

The frontpage of Campfyre includes every post with the exception of posts tagged with '#bonfyre' and posts marked as offensive/nsfw (unless you have chosen to see those posts) in chronological order.
