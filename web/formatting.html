Formatting Guide:

  • Links will automatically highlight if you enter them with an "http://". Example: http://example.com
  • Attach a link by putting it in the attachment text box. If your link is a video or an image it can embed into the post.
  • Only images uploaded to Imgur and Puush will embed.
  • Only videos from YouTube will automatically embed.
  • Hashtags are supported. A space/full stop (period)/comma/colon/question mark will end a hashtag.
  • HTML/Markdown/BBCode will not work.
If you're unsure about something, feel free to ask!
