Formatting Guide:

  • Links will automatically highlight if you enter them with an "http://". Example: http://example.com

  • Images will automatically embed if you provide a link to the "http://i.imgur.com/1MCVnW0.png" page, or the "http://sharepic.tk/i/example.jpg" page, in the attachment textbox. Only images uploaded to Imgur or Sharepic.tk will embed

  • Videos from YouTube will automatically embed into the post if you post the desktop link in the attachment textbox

  • Only put english letters in #hashtags. Numbers will not work in hashtags. Hashtags are one word only (no spaces)

  • HTML/Markdown/BBCode will not work

    • If you're unsure about something feel free to ask!
